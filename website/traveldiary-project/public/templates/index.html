Let's find a destination you could fall in love with!

Travel Diary is a web application that will help you plan and share your vacations. We offer you a community of experienced vacationists and travel bloggers that build the foundation for a hassle free vacation for you. Their knowledge about various places and fun activities is shared on our site through vacation plans and reviews. Based on this you can plan your own vacation and share your expierence with others.

Is Travel Diary for me?

Travel Diary is aimed at anyone who likes to travel without hassle. That includes for example:

  • Travel Bloggers who like to share their experience
  • Inexperienced travellers who would like to profit from the experience of others

    • Why should I use Travel Diary

    First an for most travel diary offers you the experience of others. Just search for some vacations and read the reviews. You will see that our community provides many helpful insights into planning your own vacation. That includes for example:

  • God or bad experiences with a certain hotel or airline
  • Tips on how to get the best seat in the opera
  • Insider info about various cities
  • The best restaurants at your destination

    • How can I plan a vacation?

    A vacation in travel diary consists of activities. These activities are created by users and include things like checking into a hotel, visiting a specific museum or going to the beach. There are numerous activites available. They are rated and reviewed by other users to give you a basic idea of whether or not they are actually fun. You can start planning your vacation by either copying an exisitng vacation and customizing the activities to your own needs or by starting from scratch if you want to do everything by yourself. When planning a vacation you can add activites which are either already part of the activity catalouge of Travel Diaory or you can create a new one if you found something fun.
